Paperless Go gives you fast access to your paperless-ngx archive from your phone.

Use it to stay on top of incoming paperwork, find the documents you need, and keep metadata organised while away from your desk.

Key features:
- sign in to your own paperless-ngx server
- review recent uploads and documents that still need attention
- search your archive and filter by tags, correspondents, and document types
- open document details and update metadata
- scan paper documents with your camera and upload them as PDFs

Paperless Go is built for people who already use paperless-ngx and want a focused mobile companion for their archive.
