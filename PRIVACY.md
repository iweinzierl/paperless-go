# Privacy Policy

Paperless Go is a mobile client for self-hosted paperless-ngx instances.

## Data We Process

Paperless Go stores the server URL, username, and password you enter so the app can sign in to your paperless-ngx server.

When you use the app, it processes the document metadata, files, and images provided by your own paperless-ngx server so you can browse, search, edit, and upload documents.

If you use document scanning, the app processes camera images locally so they can be converted and uploaded to your paperless-ngx server.

## Where Data Goes

Paperless Go communicates directly with the paperless-ngx server you configure. The app does not operate a separate cloud backend for your documents.

The app may open external GitHub pages for documentation and issue reporting when you explicitly request support.

## Data Sharing

Paperless Go does not sell your personal data.

Your document data is only sent to the paperless-ngx server you configure and to platform services that are necessary for app distribution and crash handling provided by Apple or Google.

## Your Choices

You can remove stored sign-in details by signing out or deleting the app data from your device.

Because Paperless Go is a client for self-hosted paperless-ngx servers, you control the server that stores your documents and metadata.
